Kale Caesar Quinoa Salad with Roasted Chicken

Kale and quinoa provide healthy base for roasted chicken topped with a light caesar souce.

Total time : 45 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup Kale.
  • 1 cup Quinoa
  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 chicken breast
  • Caesar Dressing.

Preparation

  1. Prepare quinoa and roast chicken until golden brown and 165 in middle.

    Time : 40 minutes

  2. Toss quinoa, chicken, Kale, and Caesar dressing until coated.

    Time : 4 minutes
